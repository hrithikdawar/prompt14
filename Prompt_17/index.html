Birds of the World

Discover the beauty and diversity of birds from around the globe.

Peacock

The peacock is known for its dazzling feathers, which create a mesmerizing display during courtship. Native to South Asia, these birds symbolize beauty and grace.

  • Scientific Name: Pavo cristatus
  • Habitat: Forests and grasslands
  • Diet: Seeds, insects, and plants

Bald Eagle

The bald eagle is a symbol of freedom and strength in the United States. With its piercing eyes and majestic wingspan, it is a true predator of the skies.

  • Scientific Name: Haliaeetus leucocephalus
  • Habitat: Near water bodies and open areas
  • Diet: Fish, small mammals, and carrion

Parrot

Known for their vibrant colors and intelligence, parrots are social birds that can mimic sounds and even human speech. They are found in tropical regions around the world.

  • Scientific Name: Psittaciformes
  • Habitat: Rainforests and woodlands
  • Diet: Fruits, seeds, and nuts

Penguin

Penguins are flightless birds that are perfectly adapted to life in the water. Found in the Southern Hemisphere, they are known for their playful waddling and sleek swimming.

  • Scientific Name: Spheniscidae
  • Habitat: Antarctic regions and nearby islands
  • Diet: Fish and krill
